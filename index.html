⚓ A NOTE FROM THE CAPTAIN

Everyone you know is using AI now. Your boss. Your friends. Your whole day runs through it. And somehow the lesson everybody took from it was: do more, do it faster, never stop.

We think that is backwards. AI should be doing the work so humans can go do what we love.

I love sailing. Out on the water there is no AI. There is wind, and it is enough. I love camping, and running, and laughing with my friends, and exploring the world, and figuring out my own life as I go. That is the point. That is what the time is for.

So Sailr sits in your meeting, catches the work, and does it, while you stay present with the actual humans in the room. Then it hands you your afternoon back.

Reclaim your life. Go create. Go explore. Go learn. Go laugh. Go sail.

Jonathan · captain, Sailr
